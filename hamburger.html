PIZZA!

Pizza is my favorite food to eat. I love the cheese, grease and all the combined flavores of the meats. But one of the most important parts for me is the sauce. I usally buy premade sauce and then play with different seasoning to change the flavor to my liking. I add pleanty of sause to my pies, nothing worst than dry pie!

Ingredents Needed

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Lots of butter
  • Garlic
  • Cheese, lots of cheese.
  • Meats, which ever you prefer

Lets Cook!

I buy premade pizza dough because i havent learned to make my own yet but use what ever you want. I start out with some butter in a bowl and through it in the microwave to liquify it. Afterwards I through a bit of garlic in the bowl with my now melted butter and mix together. Then i get out my silicon brush and cover the pizza dough in my butter garlic mixture.

Next i add my pizza sauce into a large bowl and add in some extra salt and pepper, garlic and whatever else i feel like adding that day. Give it a few taste test, maybe bother the wife or kids till they taste it, then once I'm happy with it its time to add it to the dough. Just grab a decent sized spoon and start smearing it around on your pie. Typically i add quite a bit of sauce to mine, cant stand a dry pie.

Time for the cheese! I spread around a generous amount of cheese untill all my sauce is covered in a nice thin layer. You can add as much as you like.

Next up meats. Depending on the type of pie im makeing i will pre cook some of my meat. Usally the sausage, ground beef or bacon if I'm using them. As for pepperoni I typically just throw it on raw.

Time to cook! Turn the oven up as high as it can go, toss your pie in, and let cook for about 15ish minutes or untill you happy with it.
